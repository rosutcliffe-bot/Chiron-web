About

Our mission is to support registered healthcare practitioners to deliver extended scope of practice interventions when operating in remote or austere environments. We can provide bespoke training packages for those providing medical cover in settings such as maritime, offshore and expedition medicine.

Privacy Policy

At Chiron Remote & Extended Care, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, or details submitted through our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiries, provide requested services, and maintain communication with you. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies required for basic functionality. These are placed without consent as they are strictly necessary. Optional analytics cookies are only activated if you explicitly click "Accept all" or enable them in the cookie preferences panel. You may withdraw consent at any time using the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process contact form data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to your enquiry). Analytics cookies are processed only on the basis of your explicit consent, in accordance with UK GDPR and PECR.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You may also request a copy of the information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

6. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and are not stored in any third-party marketing database.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at will.sutcliffe@chironremote.com.
